TITLE: FINE ARTS BACHELOR IN AUDIOVISUAL

### Bachelor in Audiovisual ###
The Faculty of Fine Arts & Architecture provides quality education in all its specializations: applied arts, plastic arts, architecture, etc. The faculty supports student artwork production in line with urban and cinematic developments and local and international plastic movements and cooperates with several Arab and international institutions.

### Faculty of Fine Arts and Architecture ###

### Bachelor ###

### Description ###
This program aims to train students in the fields of film and television production, including directing, cinematography, writing, editing, production, and documentary filmmaking.
The program includes theoretical study and practical application of the arts of film and television, with a focus on developing creative and technical skills, with the aim of preparing professionals capable of creating distinctive visual and audio content that takes into account modern production techniques and the impact of visual media on society and culture.
Provided in Branches 1 and 2
