TITLE: FINE ARTS MASTER IN INTERIOR DESIGN
========================================


### Professional Master in Interior Design ###
The Faculty of Fine Arts & Architecture provides quality education in all its specializations: applied arts, plastic arts, architecture, etc. The faculty supports student artwork production in line with urban and cinematic developments and local and international plastic movements and cooperates with several Arab and international institutions.

### Faculty of Fine Arts and Architecture ###

### Professional Master ###

### Description ###
This program includes practical and theoretical courses that deepen students' knowledge and skills acquired during their bachelor's degree and develop their analytical and design abilities through working on general projects (commercial, residential, recreational, tourism, etc.). It also analyzes human behavior to make the study of interior space more appropriate for the required function and respectful of the surrounding environmental and social conditions.
This professional master's program culminates in the design and study of a public project based on theory, which is presented by the student for discussion by a specialized committee.
Provided in Branches 1, 2, 3 and 4
Language: Arabic, French and English
