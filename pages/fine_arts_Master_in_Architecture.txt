TITLE: FINE ARTS MASTER IN ARCHITECTURE
========================================


### Professional Master in Architecture ###
The Faculty of Fine Arts & Architecture provides quality education in all its specializations: applied arts, plastic arts, architecture, etc. The faculty supports student artwork production in line with urban and cinematic developments and local and international plastic movements and cooperates with several Arab and international institutions.

### Faculty of Fine Arts and Architecture ###

### Professional Master ###

### Description ###
The education system in this master's program is based on a set of scientific and professional knowledge paths that enable students to delve deeper into the sciences and elements of architectural projects through:
Understanding and producing architectural projects through specialized workshops, defining project data within a specific geographical and social framework, and upgrading the treatment of architectural issues from a guided track to a research track.
Learning the rules of technical engineering sciences and their applications in the architectural construction industry.
In the final semester of this master's program, students undergo a practical training period of no less than six weeks as part of the “professional training” course. They are required to submit a detailed report on their training, in addition to a research report on their graduation project, which will be discussed by a specialized committee.
Provided in Branches 1, 2, 3 and 4
