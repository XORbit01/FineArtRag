TITLE: FINE ARTS BACHELOR IN ARCHITECTURE
========================================


### Bachelor in Architecture ###
The Faculty of Fine Arts & Architecture provides quality education in all its specializations: applied arts, plastic arts, architecture, etc. The Faculty supports student artwork production in line with urban and cinematic developments and local and international plastic movements, and cooperates with several Arab and international institutions.

### Faculty of Fine Arts and Architecture ###

### Bachelor ###

### Description ###
This program enables students to understand the various factors that influence architectural projects through:
Learn the fundamentals of architecture through theoretical, cultural, and specialized courses.
Understand, apply, and produce architectural projects and test multiple concepts derived from parallel sciences.
Learn and apply fine arts with the aim of developing the artistic sensibility necessary for architectural creativity.
Learn the rules of technical engineering sciences involved in the construction of architectural structures.
In the second academic year, students undergo a mandatory professional training period outside the faculty for a minimum of six consecutive weeks.
Provided in Branches 1, 2, 3 and 4
Language: Arabic, French and English
