TITLE: FINE ARTS MASTER IN LANDSCAPE AND ENVIRONMENTAL ARCHITECTURE

### Specialized Professional Master in Landscape and Environmental Architecture ###
The Faculty of Fine Arts & Architecture provides quality education in all its specializations: applied arts, plastic arts, architecture, etc. The faculty supports student artwork production in line with urban and cinematic developments and local and international plastic movements and cooperates with several Arab and international institutions.

### Faculty of Fine Arts and Architecture ###

### Master ###

### Description ###
The master’s degree in Landscape, Urban and Environmental Design Engineering (MAPE) is a multidisciplinary program focused on professional and research training.
The program aims to prepare professionals specializing in landscape engineering, combining theoretical, technical, and practical concepts to understand environmental and spatial dynamics.
The program integrates contemporary issues such as the circular economy, green cities, and sponge cities with the use of advanced technological solutions in environmental planning.
At the beginning of the 3rd semester, students begin their graduation project from among the following topics:
Landscape Planning
Landscape and Urban Design
Landscape and Rural Areas
Research Track
Provided in Branch 1
