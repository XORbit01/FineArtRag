TITLE: FINE ARTS BACHELOR IN INTERIOR DESIGN
========================================


### Bachelor in Interior Design ###
The Faculty of Fine Arts & Architecture provides quality education in all its specializations: applied arts, plastic arts, architecture, etc. The faculty supports student artwork production in line with urban and cinematic developments and local and international plastic movements and cooperates with several Arab and international institutions.

### Faculty of Fine Arts and Architecture ###

### Bachelor ###

### Description ###
This program includes practical and theoretical courses that provide students with technical knowledge and skills that help them refine their intellectual and analytical abilities in proposing and handling engineering projects. It also enables them to skillfully use and employ materials and spaces in residential, commercial, tourism, or entertainment engineering projects.
The key courses taught in this major include engineering drawing, technical drawing, architectural representation, models, implementation details, history of architecture, and history of furniture.
Provided in Branches 1, 2, 3 and 4
