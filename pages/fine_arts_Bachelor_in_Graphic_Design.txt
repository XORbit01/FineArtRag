TITLE: FINE ARTS BACHELOR IN GRAPHIC DESIGN
========================================


### Bachelor in Graphic Design ###
The Faculty of Fine Arts & Architecture provides quality education in all its specializations: applied arts, plastic arts, architecture, etc. The faculty supports student artwork production in line with urban and cinematic developments and local and international plastic movements and cooperates with several Arab and international institutions.

### Faculty of Fine Arts and Architecture ###

### Bachelor ###

### Description ###
This major enables student to acquire the basic skills needed to create materials designed to convey specific visual messages to a specific audience. These visual products often serve social, cultural, economic, or commercial purposes and take the form of visual identities, publications, books, posters, brochures and flyers, cards, covers and packaging, illustrations, comic strips, websites, interactive mobile applications, video games, animations, 2D or 3D products, marketing platforms, and display interfaces.
Provided in Branches 1, 2, 3 and 4
Language: Arabic, French and English
