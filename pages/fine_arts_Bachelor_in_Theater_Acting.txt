### Description ###
This program focuses on developing students' technical and creative skills in the field of theater acting. It includes the study of acting theories, performance techniques, theatrical text analysis, and directing, in addition to practical training on stage.
It also aims to prepare professional actors who are capable of artistic expression and effective communication with the audience, taking into account contemporary trends in the performing arts.
Provided in Branches 1 and 2
