TITLE: FINE ARTS BACHELOR IN PLASTIC ARTS
========================================


### Bachelor in Plastic Arts ###
The Faculty of Fine Arts & Architecture provides quality education in all its specializations: applied arts, plastic arts, architecture, etc. The faculty supports student artwork production in line with urban and cinematic developments and local and international plastic movements and cooperates with several Arab and international institutions.

### Faculty of Fine Arts and Architecture ###

### Bachelor ###

### Description ###
This major connects students to the Lebanese and international art scene and provides them with the necessary support to deepen their knowledge and develop their independent artistic identity.
Modern techniques and media (computer graphics, animation, web design, etc.) form the core of this major, which also includes instruction in traditional tools and languages (drawing, sculpture, stained glass, murals, photography, etc.).
Theoretical courses give students the ability to analyze and comment on works of art, while practical training allows them to master traditional and modern artistic tools (painting and sculpture using traditional and digital media).
Provided in Branches 1, 2, 3 and 4
Language: Arabic, French and English
