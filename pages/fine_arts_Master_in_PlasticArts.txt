TITLE: FINE ARTS MASTER IN PLASTICARTS
========================================


### Research Master in Plastic Arts ###
The Faculty of Fine Arts & Architecture provides quality education in all its specializations: applied arts, plastic arts, architecture, etc. The faculty supports student artwork production in line with urban and cinematic developments and local and international plastic movements and cooperates with several Arab and international institutions.

### Faculty of Fine Arts and Architecture ###

### Research Master ###

### Description ###
This master's program enhances students' theoretical and practical skills to understand the behaviors of the visual arts and embody them in visual works (sculpture, painting, photography, etc.) that take into account aesthetic standards through the adaptation of colors, spaces, lines, and more.
The program of this specialized track contributes to the formation of a mature and open critical personality in students through the study of plastic arts schools in detail and through research and professional workshops that keep pace with modernity in the field.
At the end of this master's program, students are required to submit a research thesis that meets all research standards for discussion by a specialized committee.
Provided in Branches 1, 2, 3 and 4
