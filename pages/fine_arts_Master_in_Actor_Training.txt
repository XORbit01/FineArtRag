TITLE: FINE ARTS MASTER IN ACTOR TRAINING
========================================


### Professional / Research Master in Actor Training ###
The Faculty of Fine Arts & Architecture provides quality education in all its specializations: applied arts, plastic arts, architecture, etc. The faculty supports student artwork production in line with urban and cinematic developments and local and international plastic movements and cooperates with several Arab and international institutions.

### Faculty of Fine Arts and Architecture ###

### Master ###

### Description ###
In this master's program, students learn about the key acting schools that shaped the 20th century and practice their methods in preparing actors and developing their stage presence.
M1 program includes practical courses focused on how to use the main techniques of preparing actors and theoretical courses covering the aesthetics of acting and how to research new issues related thereto.
M2 program includes courses on the work of actors in modern and contemporary experiences, while the final graduation project is supposed to be a theatrical work of at least 40 minutes prepared by the student themselves, demonstrating their skills in preparing and directing actors on stage.
This program also includes a research component that requires students to write a thesis of at least 70 pages addressing new research issues related to acting.
Provided in Branch 2
